PEAK PlasmaBlade is a recent and distinctive type of electrosurgical device.
Previous studies have already documented some meaningful advantages of this device over conventional electrosurgery.
This study compared the use of PEAK PlasmaBlade to standard electrosurgery in mastectomy and breast conservative surgery.
The purpose was to test the impact of PEAK PlasmaBlade on the wound-healing process and on postsurgical complications in breast cancer surgery.
